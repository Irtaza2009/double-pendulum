Made for **[Accelerate](http://accelerate.hackclub.com/)**, a YSWS hosted by **[Hack Club](https://hackclub.com/)**!

## Features
- **Cursor Blocks**: Turn your *cursor* into a **concrete** or **rubber** block, and *interact with the pendulum*!

- **Drag and Drop**: You can **drag and drop** the pendulum anywhere!

- **Air Resistance**: Turn on **damping** and adjust **air resistance** to see its effect on the pendulum's motion!

- **Adjustable Parameters**: You can adjust all key parameters like **gravity**, **rod lengths**, **air resistance**, and **pendulum mass**!
